FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website? +
Most websites are completed within 3–7 business days. Complex projects may take 10–14 days. We'll give you an accurate timeline after your initial consultation.
How many revisions are included? +
We include up to 3 revision rounds in every project. Minor changes are addressed within 5–7 days post-delivery at no extra cost.
Do you provide hosting and domain? +
We offer free GitHub Pages deployment for static websites. For custom hosting and domain setup, we can guide you or handle it for an additional fee.
Will my website be mobile-friendly? +
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across all major devices and screen sizes.
Do you offer SEO services? +
Yes! We include on-page SEO (titles, meta tags, semantic HTML, structured headings) with every website. Advanced SEO packages are available separately.
What technologies do you use? +
We primarily work with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and deploy via GitHub Pages. We tailor the tech stack to each project's needs.
Can I request a custom template? +
Yes. All our templates can be customized to match your brand colors, content, and requirements. Contact us for a custom quote.
How do I pay for my project? +
We accept 50% advance payment before starting and the remaining 50% upon project completion. Payment details are shared during onboarding.
